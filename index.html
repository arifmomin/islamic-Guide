The 5 pillars of Islam

Shahada

The belief that "There is no god but God, and Muhammad is the Messenger of God" is central to Islam.

Salah

Muslims pray facing Mecca five times a day: at dawn, noon, mid-afternoon, sunset, and after dark religious topic

Sawm

Sawm (Fasting) - The Fourth Pillar of Islam | Islamic Relief UK Sawm is the Arabic term used to describe the act of fasting.

Zakat

In accordance with Islamic law, Muslims donate a fixed portion of their income to community members in need.

Hajj

Every Muslim whose health and finances permit it must make at least one visit to the holy city of Mecca, in present-day Saudi
